glasgowCoderCollective

So what's the collective all about then!?


Amongst the bustling tech scene in Glasgow, lives the glasgowCoderCollective.

We're building a social community of adult coders at all levels of experience to do code related things together!



Our history

We originated as a bunch of coders from freeCodeCamp eager to meet and code together, thus glaWebDev was promptly born! Some 6months later, we began a period of rebranding to incorporate all coding interests and formed the glasgowCoderCollective.



When and where?

We meet weekly on Thurdays evenings at GCU library 6.30pm - 9. That doesn't mean everyone turns up of course or stays for the duration, so drop in when it suits you!

Some weeks we fancy a change and do a Collective outing to other tech events.

In April 2018 we hosted our first talk in collaboration with BBC Scotland, it was epic and marks the start of larger events!



Um, so I'm still not clear what to do at a meet!

Whatever you want really, instead of sitting at home coding, come along and do it socially! Here's some other ideas, just bring your laptop though!:

  • Progress through freeCodeCamp / codeAcademy / Treehouse tutorials
  • Do some of your own projects
  • Browse and talk geeky stuff
  • Listen to the comedy act that is Ed and Euan
  • Ask if anyone has a clue how to solve that thing you're stuck on!
  • Plug your earphones in for a bit to concentrate!


So come along, beginners and experts alike, join us and have fun!
