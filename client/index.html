About us


We are the glasgowCoderCollective. We meet weekly to code socially and chat about all things code related. We’re a collection of like-minded technology enthusiasts that’s open to people of all (and no) experience levels. We believe programming is most productive and fun when done collectively.


While we have our origins in web development via FreeCodeCamp we encourage anyone to come along. Think of us like a big group of friends, chatting about code, tech articles, showing the latest in problematic programmes, troublesome tutorials and sometimes people actually just code!!


We meet on Thursday evenings 6.30pm onwards in the library of Glasgow Caledonian University, and you can turn up for as much of the evening as you want. Bring your laptop if you want to code or drop by for coding chat.

Bring something to eat if you want. Although we'd like to get sponsorship and join the ranks of other tech meets with pizza, for now we bring our own. There are vending machines for sweets, cold/hot drinks, otherwise there's a Gregg's in Buchanan Bus Station and a Sainsbury's adjacent to it.
